Why Rush Theta Tau Zeta Delta?

Profession

Coming from all types of fields and backgrounds, our brothers' work experience and connections last a lifetime. Their knowledge is always passed down to younger members that wish to follow a similar path. We have a professional development chair, who is dedicated to helping all of our members gain the professional experience they need to help them beyond their time at the University of South Carolina. Brotherhood

We at Theta Tau Zeta Delta host many brotherhood events to strengthen bonds and create lasting memories. From beach and mountain retreats, we build friendships that last a lifetime.

Service

Here at Theta Tau Zeta Delta we work to make an impact in our community every semester. From STEM outreach events to community service projects, we are dedicated to making a difference in our community.
