Anticipated Fall 2024 Semester Rush Events:

Info Nights

A time to gather and meet brothers to immerse yourself into what Theta Tau is really about. Starts off with a presentation from our Recruitment Chairs to welcome you to the info night and tell you more about who we are as a fraternity. Opens the floor to questions as well as free pizza provided to allow brothers and rushees to chit chat and learn more about us as we get to know more about you!

Philanthropy and Committee Fair

This event will help you understand how our committee system works and what each of our chairs do. This will give you some time to talk to chairs to understand the involvement they play in the fraternity. On the other hand, philanthropy will be providing some different tasks they need help with while preparing for Fall Into Science.

Professional Development Workshop

This is an event to really dive into your professional skills in a more relaxed environment. We will walk through some changes to be made on resumes, LinkedIn learning, mock interviews, research chats, etc. This event is business casual

Olympic Trials

Come hang out with some brothers while being involved in some games set up around the horseshoe (RAIN MIGHT CHANGE TO SWEARINGEN). This event will be a lot of different activities but wear comfortable clothes and get ready to join the trials!

Speed Dating

A required event that allows rushees to interact with each brother through quick conversations. Be prepared with a water bottle as the speed dating event typically lasts longer. Speed dating will allow us to learn more about you and take this time to meet other rushees as this event will typically have some wait time based on the number of rushees.

Professional Dinner (Invite Only)

An invite only event that will help us test your professional skills. This will start out with a dinner conversation and then lead into different rotations. More to be explained at the event. This event is for business professional.
