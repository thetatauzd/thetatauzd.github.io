Anticipated Fall 2024 Semester Rush Events:

Info Nights

Join as we kick off rush where you’ll get an in-depth introduction to Theta Tau and our fraternity’s values. The event begins with a presentation by our Recruitment Chairs, offering insights into who we are and what we stand for. Following the presentation, there will be an open Q&A session, providing an opportunity to ask questions and learn more. Enjoy complimentary pizza while networking with our brothers and fellow attendees in a casual and welcoming environment. Attire is casual and both info nights are the same event so you only should attend one night.

Philanthropy and Committee Fair

Discover the inner workings of Theta Tau at our Philanthropy and Committee Fair. This event highlights our chair and committee system, offering you the chance to interact and ask questions to the chairs directly. Additionally, our philanthropy team will provide tasks and activities in preparation for Living Science Museum, allowing you to get involved and gain a deeper understanding of our community impact.

Professional Development Workshop

Refine your professional skills at our Professional Development Workshop. This workshop covers essential topics such as resume improvement, LinkedIn optimization, and mock interviews, as well as research and internship panels to elevate your career readiness. Attendees should dress in business casual attire as we work together to build and bolster your professional profile.

Professional Mixer

Event in which brothers and rushees can network and get to know one another is a low stakes casual, professional manner. The attire for this event is business casual.

Speed Dating

A pivotal event in the recruitment process, Speed Dating offers an opportunity for one-on-one interactions with each brother through a series of quick conversations. This format allows us to learn more about you while giving you the chance to connect with others in attendance. Please come prepared with a water bottle, as this event typically lasts for an extended period and may involve some waiting time. This event is mandatory to be considered for a bid.

Professional Dinner (Invite Only)

This exclusive event is designed to showcase your professionalism and engagement. The evening begins with a formal dinner conversation, followed by interactive rotations that further explore your skills and potential. Additional details will be provided before the event. Business professional attire is required for all attendees.
