Theta Tau Southeast Regionals 2022

Columbia, SC

Zeta Delta chapter at the University of South Carolina is honored to be hosting the Southeastern Regional Conference on September 10th, 2022, in Columbia, SC. More details will be coming later, such as schedules and breakout rooms, but anybody can already register!

If you pay before July 1st, you’ll pay the early registration fee of $25, with the price moving up to $35 before August 1st, and $45 before September 1st.

Contact Lauren Speck, Regent and Regionals Chair, if you have any questions.
lspeck@email.sc.edu

Registration Information

  1. Please fill out this Google Form with your information.
  2. Go to https://pyli.app/tickets/217
  3. Select either the full pass or day pass.
  4. Enter number of passes and hit Attend/Checkout.
  5. Choose payment option and checkout.

Workshop Information

If your chapter is interested in leading any workshops, please contact Zeta Delta’s Regionals Chair, Lauren Speck, at lspeck@email.sc.edu These workshops can be related to professional development, or just any special thing your chapter is great at and would want to share with the rest of the region. These could be about fundraising, alumni relations, outreach, service, or anything else you think could be interesting!
