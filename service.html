STEM Outreach Events

Making a difference in the lives of local students through STEM education and hands-on learning.

Theta Tau hosts two STEM outreach events a year for local K-12 students, where students are exposed to engineering and science in a fun and engaging way.

Each event involves months of planning, community engagement, and technical preparation. Brothers collaborate with local schools and community leaders to ensure our impact reaches as many students as possible.
